The Rotary Clubs of Bermuda Welcome You

Bermuda is part of District 7230 which also covers New York City, Bronx, Staten Island, and Westchester County.

There are 4 clubs in Bermuda, one lunch, two dinner and one breakfast. If you are visiting Bermuda please come and see us.

Click on the Club name below to visit their web site.
